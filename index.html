Camel

Chameleon

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  2. Changing skin colour is an importantpart of communication among chameleons

Panda

  • Pandas live in China
  • Pandas weigh 4-8 ounces at Birth and eat 25-40 Pounds per day
  • Pandas are amazing at climbing trees

Bear

  1. Bears are extraordinarily intelligent animals
  2. Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing
  3. Bears have large canine teeth to aid in opening logs to look for insects

Pigeon

  • Pigeons are highly complex and intelligent animals
  • Pigeons are reowned for their outstanding navigation abilities
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Butterfly

  1. Butterflies live on an All-Liquid Diet
  2. Some Butterflies are carnivores
